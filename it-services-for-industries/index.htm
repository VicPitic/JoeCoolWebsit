Getting to know your idea

Scoping & estimation

First things first! We identify the scope of your project and take our time to understand your vision and business expectations. This means discussing potential project features, deciding on the right tech solutions and the time required to deliver them. Thanks to that, we can give you a general development quotation and schedule.

Defining your vision and discussing the details

Workshops & preparation

We want to make sure your idea is achieved successfully. To help you choose the right strategy and sharpen your vision, we offer sessions of Data, Product and Strategy workshops – whatever best fits your needs. At the end of this phase, we have wireframes of your app and the first design drafts, we’ve gathered development requirements, and prepared the basic documentation of the project. All set!

Transforming your idea into a real product

Design & development

Time to get to work! Once the project kicks off, we complete the designs, and start creating your app. We encourage you to be fully engaged throughout the project, as we see you as our development partner. The length of this phase varies depending on the app size and complexity, but it usually takes us 3 months to create a fully-functional MVP.

Bringing your product to the market

Product release

Time to shine! As soon as your product is ready, we take care of the deployment and release. Timing is crucial here: we make certain that every individual part of the system is released on schedule and works flawlessly, without any downtime. If it’s a mobile app, we take care of the app store listings as well. We also provide ongoing support after your app goes live.

Taking good care of your product

Product growth

Our work doesn’t have to end once the MVP is released. At Miquido, we put special focus on building long-term relationships with our clients, so if there are any additional features you’d like your product to have, or some enhancements or touch ups that you’ve come up with – we’ve got your back. Let’s ensure your product’s growth together!
